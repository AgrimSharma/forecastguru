FAQs

What is Forecast Guru

Forecast Guru (FG) is a decentralized platform for prediction markets. The events of FG’s prediction markets will be chosen by members that hold FG's native FG points token, who stake their tokens on the event.

How does the prediction marketplace, Forecast Guru, Work

Members can make forecasts or participate in live forecast events created by other members. To participate in a live forecast event, members need to use their FG points token and make a YES or NO forecast. If the member’s forecast is correct, they will earn FG points from the pool of GF points in play for the respective forecast. If the member’s forecast is incorrect, they will lose all the FG points staked on the forecast event. FG's incentive structure is designed to ensure that a large pool of popular events are created and in-play at any given point in time.

Who makes Forecasts
Any member can make a forecast. All forecasts are first submitted to Admin for approval. Once approved, the forecast is published on the Forecast.Guru website.

All members who create forecasts are entitled to a ‘market fee’ which is currently 5% of the total FG points in play.

What are FG points
FG points or “Forecast Guru” Points is a virtual token with which all forecasts are played. This token has no monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash or gifts.
How do I earn FG points
You earn FG points by winning or correctly predicting forecasts and by creating forecasts (in which case you are entitled to a part of the ‘market fee’)
What is FG points “Win Ratio”
The Win Ratio defines the FG points a member can win for every 1 FG point staked on a valid forecast, if the member was to predict the correct result for the said forecast. The ratio is defined as 1:x where “x” stands for the number of FG points the member will win i.e. a win ratio of 1:3 would imply that the member will get 3 FG points against each FG point staked on a forecast, if the member correctly predicts the result of that forecast. *Please note that this includes the 1 FG point staked and is subject to market fees payout.
How is the “Win Ratiao” decided
Win Ratio is decided by the number of FG staked on both outcomes i.e. Yes and No. For example, if a forecast has 100 FG points stakes on ‘Yes’ outcome and 200 FG points staked on ‘No’ outcome, then the win ratio for ‘Yes’ outcome is 1:3 , whereas the win ratio for the ‘No outcome’ is 1:1.5

Whats I ‘Market Fee’
